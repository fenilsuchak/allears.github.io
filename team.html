What most people really need is a good listening to.
—Mary Lou Casey

Empathy is not simply a combination of letters but one of the fundamental traits that makes us human. At All Ears, we envision a society where empathy is infused into everyday life, one that fosters it. Sometimes, letting someone know the depth of your empathy by being there, by being a sympathetic sounding board for their emotions, is all that’s needed for the moment. They are not helpless. Just a bit faltered and discouraged, but not helpless. We believe that pursuit of acts of kindness and empathy are fundamental for the achievement of Sustainable Development Goals, and for building peaceful and supportive societies.
